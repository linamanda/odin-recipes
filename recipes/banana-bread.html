Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is one of the most classic sweet treats. Sweetened and moistened with overripe bananas, this dessert gives the allure of a slightly healthier version of a loaf cake. Dense, but tender, soft, and moist - it's sure to be a treat whether you're looking for a last minute quick bake to bring to a get-together, or if you're looking to savor it all yourself!

This exact recipe comes from the JustOneCookbook blog. It is a recipe that has been tried a countless number of times. If you're looking for a gluten-free version, this recipe has proven to be just as good! All you need to do is replace the recipe's flour with a 1-to-1 gluten-free flour.

Ingredients

  • 200 g all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 tsp kosher salt
  • 0.5 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • 113 g unsalted butter (softened to room temperature)
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 large eggs (at room temperature)
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 30 g raisins (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients. The eggs must be at room temperature.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350째F (180째C). Line a loaf baking pan with parchment paper.
  3. In a fine-mesh sieve (or sifter) over a bowl, combine 200 g all-purpose flour (plain flour), 0.5 tsp baking soda, 0.5 tsp baking powder, 0.5 tsp Diamond Crystal kosher salt, and sift all together.
  4. In the bowl of a stand mixer, beat 113 g unsalted butter (softened) until creamy.
  5. Add 100 g sugar and whisk until well blended, fluffy, and pale yellow.
  6. Add 2 large eggs (50 g each w/o shell) and beat well.
  7. Using a silicone spatula, scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl and continue beating.
  8. Add 1 Tbsp neutral oil and 1 tsp pure vanilla extract. Beat until well blended.
  9. Mash 3 ripe bananas and stir to blend with the egg mixture.
  10. Add the flour mixture to the banana mixture and stir just until 80% blended. Add 80 g raisins at this stage, if you'd like to include them, and blend for 3 seconds.
  11. While the white specs of flour are still visible, remove the mixing bowl from the stand mixer. Using the silicone spatula, hand-blend the rest of the flour. This will avoid overmixing the batter.
  12. Pour the batter into the prepared pan, spreading it evenly with the silicone spatula.
  13. Bake at 350째F (180째C) until a toothpick inserted into the center of the bread comes out clean, about 50-60 minutes.
  14. Take out the baking pan from the oven and let cool for 45-60 minutes. Remove the banana bread from the baking pan.
